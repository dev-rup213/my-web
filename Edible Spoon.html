Edible Spoons

Overview:

The edible spoon is designed as a sustainable alternative to plastic spoons , offering a balance between durability and usability. It can br used with a varity of food types and consumed after use or disposed of naturally.

Ingredients:

  • Wheat Flour
  • Rice Flour
  • Salt
  • Water

Design & Structure

  • Medium thickness for optimal strength
  • Smooth surface for comfortable use
  • Maintains shape during normal eating duration

Usage:

  • Sustainable for rice,deserts,curries,and semi-liquid foods
  • Can Handles both hot and cold items.

Performance

  • Retains Structure for 15-20 minutes in semi-liquid foods.
  • Does not break easily under normal usage
  • After use can be eaten directly
