Edible Fork

Overview:

The Edible fork is engineered for solid food hadling, providing enough rigidity a pierce and lift food while remaining Eco-friendly and consumable.

Ingredients:

  • Wheat Flour
  • Rice Flour
  • Salt
  • Water

Design & Structure

  • Reinforced prongs for better grip
  • Slightly denser composition for added strength.
  • Designed to prevent bending during use.

Usage:

  • Best suited for solid foods like pasta,fruits,snacks.
  • Not recommended for heavy cutting or very hard items.

Performance

  • Retains Structure for 15-20 minutes in semi-liquid foods.
  • Does not break easily under normal usage
  • After use can be eaten directly
